Jean Grey is intimately associated with the Phoenix, an immensely powerful cosmic entity.[22] After being corrupted by the Hellfire Club, Phoenix rose as the Dark Phoenix and caused immeasurable destruction.
Tony Stark is a genius inventor and billionaire industrialist, who suits up in his armor of cutting-edge technology to become the super hero Iron Man.
Wanda Maximoff, the Scarlet Witch, is a Romani sorceress and twin sister of the speedster Quicksilver.[5][27][3] Once believed to have been mutant children of Magneto,[28] the twins are in truth orphans enhanced by the High Evolutionary at Mount Wundagore in Transia.
